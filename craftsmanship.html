The Peace Surfers Way

Made by Hand,
Shaped by the Sea

Every piece we craft at Peace Surfers begins not on a production line, but with a feeling — the weight of a wave, the pull of a current, the quiet after a clean ride. Our goods are shaped by function and finished by hand, drawing on years of ocean experience and a deep respect for the materials we work with. This is not mass production. This is intentional making, one piece at a time, for surfers who know the difference.

Craft That Earns Its Place in the Lineup

From our surf store workshop to the water's edge — each product is tested, refined, and built to last beyond a single season.

THE CRAFT

From Raw Material
to Ready Ride

Every Peace Surfers product passes through four deliberate stages — no shortcuts, no compromises. Just honest handmade work built to last in the water.

01
Sourcing

Materials That Earn Their Place

We start at the source — selecting only natural fibres, sustainably harvested woods, and ocean-safe waxes from suppliers who share our values. At our surf store, every raw ingredient is handled and assessed by hand before it ever meets a shaping tool. If it doesn't feel right, it doesn't make the cut.

02
Shaping

Formed by Hand, Not Machine

Shaping is where intention becomes form. Our craftspeople work slowly and deliberately, reading the grain, feeling the resistance, and adjusting with each pass. No two pieces are identical — that variance is the signature of genuine handcraft. The result is a product with a character you can hold in your hands.

03
Finishing

Sealed for Salt, Sun & Time

Finishing is the stage most people never see — and the one that determines how long a product endures. We sand, seal, and treat each piece with natural protective coatings that resist saltwater, UV exposure, and the general violence of life in the surf. Every edge is checked. Every surface is earned.

Salt-Resistant Seal

Natural beeswax and plant-oil coating on all exposed surfaces

UV Protection

Treated to withstand prolonged sun and glare at the break

Edge Integrity

Hand-checked seams and corners for lasting durability

04
Field Testing

Proven in the Water

Before anything reaches our innerlight surf shop floor, it goes in the ocean. Real surfers, real conditions — cold mornings, overhead sets, rocky entries. We believe a product earns its place through performance, not promises. Only what survives the water makes it to you.

Raw Materials

Crafted From
the Earth &
the Ocean

Every Peace Surfers piece begins with an honest material — natural fibers that breathe, weathered woods that age beautifully, waxed cloth that repels salt and sun. We source for longevity, tactility, and respect for the craft. This is what separates a handmade surf store from the rest.

Selected for surf life. Built to age with grace.

Natural Fibers

We work with organic cotton, hemp, and linen — raw, unbleached, and sourced from small-batch suppliers who share our values. These fibers wear in rather than wear out, developing character with every session and every wash. The wavelength surf shop community deserves materials that feel as good as the ocean itself.

Weathered Wood & Waxed Cloth

Reclaimed timber and cold-processed waxed canvas form the structural backbone of our boards, bags, and accessories. The wood carries the memory of the sea. The waxed cloth hardens against spray and softens against skin — a paradox only found in materials that have earned their place in the surf store lineage.

Durable Fastenings & Tactile Finishes

Solid brass rivets, hand-stitched waxed thread, and cold-forged buckles hold everything together — literally and philosophically. We finish surfaces by hand: sanded smooth, oiled or waxed, never painted over. At our hobies surf shop-inspired atelier, the finish is the story. Run your fingers across it and you'll know it was made by someone who surfs.

What We Stand For

Built on Conviction,
Shaped by the Sea

At Peace Surfers, every handmade product carries a philosophy. These aren't just words — they're the principles that guide every wax, every stitch, and every board we craft. Like any good surf store worth its salt, we believe what you stand on reflects who you are.

01

Authenticity Over Mass Production

We make things by hand because machines can't feel the ocean. Each product that leaves our workshop is touched, shaped, and finished by someone who surfs — someone who understands what the water demands. No factory floors, no assembly lines, no compromise. Just honest craft made for real surfers.

02

Longevity Over Throwaway Culture

We're tired of gear that dies after a season. Everything we create is built to outlast trends, endure salt and sun, and age with character rather than decay. When you invest in a Peace Surfers piece, you're choosing something that earns its patina — a companion for years of sessions, not just a few weekends.

03

Community Over Trend Chasing

Surf culture was never meant to be a marketing category. It's a living, breathing community of people drawn together by the same pull of the tide. We build for that community — the dawn patrol regulars, the weekend warriors, the kids learning to pop up for the first time. Not for the algorithm. Not for the season drop.
