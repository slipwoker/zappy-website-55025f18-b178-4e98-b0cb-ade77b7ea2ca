Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Why does my handmade product look slightly different from the photos?

Every piece at Peace Surfers is crafted by hand — shaped, stitched, or finished one at a time by skilled artisans who love the ocean as much as you do. Natural materials, hand-applied finishes, and the organic process of making mean no two items are ever identical. These subtle variations in grain, texture, or tone are not flaws — they're proof of genuine craft. Your piece is truly one of a kind, the same way no two waves are ever the same.

How quickly will you respond to my message or custom order inquiry?

We're a small, passionate crew — not a faceless surf store chain. We read every message personally and aim to reply within 1–2 business days. For custom requests, partnership ideas, or wholesale inquiries, allow a little extra time so we can give your question the thoughtful answer it deserves. Drop us a line through the contact form or reach us directly by email, and we'll get back to you as soon as the tide allows.

Can you help me find the right product or explore a collaboration?

Absolutely — that's what we're here for. Whether you're hunting for the perfect board bag, a handmade wax comb, or a gift for a fellow surfer, we're happy to guide you toward the right piece. We also love connecting with shapers, artists, photographers, and surf communities who share our values. Tell us what you have in mind and let's see if we can make something happen together. Real surfers, real conversations — no bots, no scripts.
